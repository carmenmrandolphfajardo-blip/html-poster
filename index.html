How to survive the Adpocalypse

The oversaturation of ads in internet spaces has reached critical mass. Companies don't even care to make their ads worthwhile when they know they can buy up as much space as they want from popular platforms. Those same platforms know that they can charge users a monthly premium for the privelege of going ad free. You have the power to opt out! Collectively we have the power to demand a better internet.

  1. Use ad blockers. There are many free ad blockers available for all major browsers. Some popular options include AdBlock, uBlock Origin, and AdGuard.
  2. Support content creators directly. Many creators rely on platforms like Patreon, Ko-fi, or direct donations to fund their work. By supporting them directly, you can help them continue to create content without relying on ad revenue.
  3. Use alternative platforms. Consider using platforms that prioritize user experience and have fewer ads, such as Vimeo for videos or DuckDuckGo for search.
  4. Provide feedback to platforms. Let the platforms you use know that you prefer an ad-free experience and that you are willing to pay for it if necessary. This can help encourage them to offer better options for users who want to avoid ads.
