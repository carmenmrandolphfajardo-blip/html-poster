How to survive:
The Adpocalypse

The oversaturation of ads in digital spaces has reached critical mass. Companies don't even care to make a thoughtfully designed ad when they know they can buy up as much space as they want from popular platforms. Those same platforms know that they can charge users a monthly premium for the privelege of going ad free. We deserve a better digital landscape! Here's how you can start to make a change:

1. Take control of your data

All those thousands of tiny interactions you have on the internet are being tracked and sold to advertisers who can then target you with personalized ads. Take that extra moment to reject tracking cookies and opt out of data collection when you can.

2. Install an Adblocker

Ublock Origin
AdBlock

3. Invest in physical media

Skip the hosting sites and own your media directly. Search your public libraries or build your own library. Buy hard copies of your favorite movies, books and music.

4. Hold corporations accountable

Support digital spaces that stay ad free and stay away from sites that have become oversaturated with ads. Tell these companies that you don't want to see ads on their platforms. Keep an eye on the legislation around data privacy and ad practices.
